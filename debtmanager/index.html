Your Finances

Nowadays, when so many different things are necessary for life, it is easy to take out a lot of loans from different banks. Also you can get a bunch of credit cards and successfully use them. And there is also a mortgage, payments for communication and Internet, utilities. Everything needs to be paid on time, and it is not clear how much money will be left to last until the next paycheck.
Sound familiar? Then this app is for you! It will help keep your finances under control. Just enter your main expected incomes and expenses and keep track of the amount you can count on. Is the payment deadline approaching? The app will notify you on the day specified.
